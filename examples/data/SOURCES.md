# Showcase data sources

The demo files in this folder are compact extracts prepared for examples/showcase.html.

- global_population_regions.csv, world_population_history.csv, top_countries_population_2023.csv: UN World Population Prospects 2024 via Our World in Data grapher endpoint population-unwpp.csv, retrieved 2026-06-04.
- age_structure_regions_2023.csv and age_distribution_regions_2023.csv: UN World Population Prospects 2024 via Our World in Data grapher endpoints population-by-age-group.csv and population-by-five-year-age-group.csv, retrieved 2026-06-04.
- country_development.csv: Vega datasets gapminder-health-income.csv, retrieved 2026-06-04. Columns are income PPP, life expectancy, population, and World Bank style region labels.

Values are rounded for a visual showcase.
